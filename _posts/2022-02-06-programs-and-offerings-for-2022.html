Thank you for being here and choosing to explore what we can do together this year. As things are ever-evolving with the times, I've been putting a lot of thought into what I can best offer my clients - present and future - and how to deliver the best of my sessions, courses and workshops so you get the most out of them.

Below you'll find three different offerings to suit different needs. Please take your time to read through and feel for where you'd like to go right now on your journey of self-exploration and wellbeing.

1:1 Sessions

A whole 90-minute session dedicated to you. One-on-one sessions include bodywork, embodied counselling and somatic explorations. Each session can look and feel different, and will be guided by what you'd like to explore. Available online from February 2022 and in-person in Sydney from March 2022.

Perfect for: Clients who prefer a personalised approach to the therapeutic process where you can explore and uncover your journey in safe and private space.

Learn More

Intensive Programme

A 3-month programme delivered via Zoom. This programme is based on my doctoral dissertation and is focused on helping you develop skills to better cope with situations, be grounded, present and develop self-compassion and capacity to regulate and find balance.

Perfect for: Anyone who is interested in a learning-oriented therapeutic process and anyone who is seeking support or a boost to their current therapeutic sessions. Also ideal for those who are not able to commit to long-term sessions at the moment but want to begin their journey towards self-exploration.

Discover More

Explorations and Self-discovery

Weekly free-of-charge self-exploration sessions to develop more embodied awareness, presence, equanimity, compassion and aliveness. You'll learn various techniques and practices to deepen these skills as well as explore aspects of your mental and emotional self. No commitment necessary. Available from March 2022.

Perfect for: Anyone new to the journey of self-discovery and self-exploration as well as those who are seeking more of a somatic approach to support their ongoing therapeutic journey.
