Feel safe in your body, develop skills to better cope with challenging situations, find grounding when life feels shaky and develop a deeper compassion for yourself as you move through life and everything it brings your way.

This is for you if you say yes to any of these:

  • You want to develop skills to be able to identify states of dysregulation or reactive emotional responses before they take over.
  • You want to bring more balance and regulation to your inner experience so you feel more responsive rather than reactive.
  • You want to deepen your understanding of yourself and how to bring balance and wellbeing to your mental and emotional health.
  • You experience states of anxiety and depressed moods, and can sometimes feel stuck in them.
  • You want to improve your relationship with yourself.
  • You are already seeking professional help and want to go deeper into the knowledge of yourself or you would like more support around your therapeutic journey.

This is not recommended for anyone who is actively experiencing severe trauma. Part of the work is unsupervised and you will need to be able to hold space for yourself and/or have support from a practitioner or trusted resource. If you are interested in exploring the topics above, please reach out about 1:1 sessions.

This programme includes:

  • 12 hours of LIVE gathering and guidance
  • 11 hours of explorations
  • ~ 10 hours of recorded teachings
  • Visual aid to help you navigate through the programme

The modality is hybrid, which includes recorded material and LIVE sessions for Q&A and reviews.

Each week, you will have 1 x session to go through (about 1 hour of reading/ listening, learning which can be split up into different blocks).

Every fortnight, we will come together for a LIVE 2 hours session.

During the programme, you are also welcome to join the Somatic Explorations Group at no additional charge.

It has helped me to lower my anxiety, and to discover that I can be safe and feel safe in my own body. It has also given me knowledge about my own body and my state of mind."

This programme was designed as part of my doctoral dissertation and the statistics from the data analysis showed that there has been a marked trend of improvement in participants' self-reported levels of anxiety and wellbeing.
The top four reported benefits: awareness of inner state of arousal, capacity to self-regulate, improved social engagement and emotional well-being.
